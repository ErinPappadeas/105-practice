Embrace the Mistake

It's okay to make mistakes, even multiple times. Making mistakes is a great way to learn how to do something the correct way, and remember how to do it.

Making mistakes is also a great thing to get used to! A big part of web development is debugging your code. To do that you have to make, find, and fix your mistakes.
